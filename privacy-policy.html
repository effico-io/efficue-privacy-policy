EffiCue

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026

Overview

EffiCue is a mobile app that helps users schedule task alarms, organize categories, and play custom alarm sounds. The app is designed to keep all alarm and task data local to the user’s device.

Data Collected

EffiCue stores the following information locally on the device:

  • Task and category titles and metadata
  • Alarm dates, times, repeat settings, and vibration preferences
  • Selected alarm sounds and uploaded custom audio file paths
  • Task and category associations for alarms

Uploaded Audio

When you upload a custom sound, the file is saved in the app’s local storage directory. These audio files are used only for alarm playback and are not shared with third parties.

Permissions

EffiCue may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications – to display alarm alerts and reminders.
  • Exact alarms – to trigger alarms at the scheduled time.
  • Media/audio access – only when importing a custom alarm sound using the file picker.

No Third-Party Tracking

EffiCue does not use analytics, advertising, or external tracking services. All functionality is designed to operate offline and on-device.

Data Retention

Your data remains on the device until you delete it from the app or uninstall EffiCue.

Contact and Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the app and website will reflect the new version. For questions, please contact the app maintainer through the project repository.
